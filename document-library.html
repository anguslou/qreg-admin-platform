Source
Securities & Futures Commission
Reference
SFC/21/145
Date Published

Document Summary

This circular sets out the regulatory requirements for virtual asset trading platform operators in Hong Kong. It covers licensing procedures, operational requirements, custody arrangements, and client protection measures. The document also outlines the SFC's approach to supervising these platforms and ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
